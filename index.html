🎛 Dashboard Controls - Start Here
The dashboard has one key filter that drives everything: Lookback Period (X days). Set this to define what counts as "dormant" - e.g. 30, 60, or 90 days. All signals below update instantly.
30-day lookback 60-day lookback 90-day lookback Custom range
📐 KPI Definitions
Churn Signal - At-Risk Value Revenue × Churn Flag Total revenue at stake from accounts who have skipped their buying cycle within the selected lookback window. These accounts haven't reordered within their expected frequency. Act now.
At-Risk Accounts (Count) COUNT(Accounts WHERE gap > X days) Number of customers who haven't placed an order in X days relative to their historical purchase frequency. Ranked by revenue size so you prioritize high-value accounts first.
Reactivation Accounts Previously dormant, 1+ order in window Accounts that were flagged as at-risk in a prior period but have now placed an order. Track these to measure recovery efforts and identify what triggered re-engagement.
Order Value (per account) SUM(Order Lines) by Account Total order value per customer in the selected period. Compare against historical average to spot accounts where spend is declining, a leading indicator of churn before full dormancy.
🧭 How to Use This Dashboard
1
Set your lookback window: Start with 60 days for most businesses. Adjust down to 30 for fast-moving accounts, up to 90 for seasonal buyers.
2
Review At-Risk accounts: Sorted by revenue. Assign your reps to the top 10 before any other outreach this week.
3
Click into any account: See their 12-month order history, category/SKU breakdown, and month-by-month trend. You'll immediately see if they've reduced orders or dropped a category.
4
Check Reactivation tab: Celebrate wins and understand what outreach worked. Use this to refine your save playbook.
5
Monthly trend + SKU growth: Use the bottom panels to see which categories are growing or declining per account, perfect for upsell conversations.
